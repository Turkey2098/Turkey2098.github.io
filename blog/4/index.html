Blocked Websites

A timeline of all blocked websites in DCS

5/13/2022

Repl:

If anyone doesn't know, Repl is a IDE (Integrated Development Enviornment). Repl was the first coding website that the coders at my school used. It lived a reasonably long life of about 9 months, but that changed when it got blocked for the first time.

On Febuary 12th, 2021, any URL ending in .repl.co was wifi blocked. Now this wasn't that big of deal because it happened when we were hybrid learning. Remember Covid? So I wait till the next day when we do in-school learning to ask them if they had the same issue, but when I go to show them, Repl.It was blocked. And our projects ending in .repl.co weren't blocked.

Now I'm getting a bit worried because I've got an english project on Repl and I can't work on it

Scratch:

I would expect everyone would know what scratch is. Whether you've coded on it for a school project, or if you've played some games on it, I bet you know what it is. If you don't, Scratch is normally the first step to coding. Most people start coding with Scratch first, because of how easy it is. Instead of typing and having to deal with files, and spelling everything right, you can drag and drop little blocks of code like, Move forward 5 steps, or turn around 180 degrees. It is normally the first step to coding.

Anyways on May 10th, 2022, DCS decided to do something big, somthing crazy, something never before seen, THEY BLOCKED SCRATCH!!!

Now, I'm not sure why they did. And I'm sure they realized there mistake because they unblocked it after a whole 2 hours. I have a feeling that a random 5th grade teacher just trying to teach some coding and was reasonably confused why it didn't work.

But wait, THERE'S MORE! 2 days later on May 12th, THEY BLOCKED IT AGAIN! And then 3 hours later, THEY UNBLOCKED IT AGAIN! I just wanna know the districts thought process for blocking scratch twice. But at least they unblocked it twice as well. ¯\_(ツ)_/¯
