A Stars Life

Randomized Choose Your Own Adventure

2/28/22

A Stars Life is a choose your own adventure game that I made in the 2nd semester of my 8th grade year. It was made for a physical science project that I said you know what, why not code it. On the website you go through different stages of a star. It is randomized meaning sometimes you could end up as a black hole, a planetary nebula, or not even make it to becoming a star. To play, just click the next button at the button and learn about stars.
